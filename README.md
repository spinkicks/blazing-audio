**Blazing Audio** is a Brilliant-style interactive learning app for home audio fundamentals. Every lesson includes hands-on interactive elements: sliders, drag-and-drop, wiring diagrams, placement tools, and more. You learn by doing, not by watching videos.

**Live site:** https://blazing-audio-alpha.web.app/

## What it covers

A structured course from sound waves to real-world setup:

- Sound waves, frequency, and frequency response
- How speakers work and how to power them safely
- Signal clipping, subwoofer placement, and wiring
- Receivers, surround sound, balanced vs. unbalanced connections
- Phase alignment, watts/decibels, and amplifier classes

Progress, streaks, and review for missed questions persist when you sign in.

## Features

- **Interactive by design:** Every lesson has problems you manipulate directly (sliders, diagrams, wiring, placement, matching, and more).
- **Instant feedback:** Specific explanations on every answer, right or wrong.
- **Adaptive path:** Resume where you left off; review topics you missed.
- **Mobile-friendly:** Works on phone-sized screens and desktop.

## Tech stack

- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Zustand
- **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Hosting)

## Local development

